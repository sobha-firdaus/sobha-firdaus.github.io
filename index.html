Payment Plan for Sobha Firdaus

Sobha Firdaus offers a thoughtfully structured payment plan designed to provide ease and flexibility for potential homeowners. The plan begins with a 10% booking fee, allowing you to secure your dream home without a significant upfront financial burden. This initial payment ensures your place in the luxurious Sobha Firdaus community.

Following the booking, the payment schedule is spread out over the construction period, making it manageable and convenient. Within the first three months of booking, a 10% installment is due. This is followed by another 10% installment at the six-month mark. To maintain a steady yet manageable financial commitment, subsequent 10% payments are due at nine months and twelve months from the booking date.

This phased approach ensures that you can comfortably plan and manage your finances while watching your future home take shape. With each milestone, you're a step closer to owning a prestigious residence in the sought-after Sobha Firdaus development. This payment plan not only offers flexibility but also aligns with the project's construction timeline, ensuring a smooth and seamless experience for every buyer.
